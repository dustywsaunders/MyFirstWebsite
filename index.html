About me

I am an Architect from Cape Town, South Africa. After completing my studies I relocated to Windhoek, Namibia; where I lived and worked for 6 years. After meeting my American fiancee in Namibia we moved to Auckland, New Zealand. In April 2018 we decided to relocate to The Netherlands.

Education

In 2010 I completed my Masters degree in Architecture at the Univeristy of Pretoria. I spent 6 years working for a commercial architecture firm in Namibia, designing and building everything from bridges to shopping centres, high rise apartments and hospitals. In Auckland I joined forces with another Architect specializing in residential developments.

Hobbies

In our spare time we love using trail running and hiking as an excuse to discover new areas and countries. I also enjoy good craft beer and spending time online with friends on Xbox.
